What Is Life Under Water?

Life under water includes all plants and animals living in oceans, seas, rivers, and lakes. This underwater world supports millions of species.

Why Is It Important?

  • Oceans produce more than half of the Earth's oxygen.
  • Marine life provides food for billions of people.
  • Oceans regulate global climate and temperature.
  • Supports millions of jobs worldwide.
