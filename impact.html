Oil Spills

Toxic chemicals harm fish and sea birds. Oil is one of them. Most of the oil fall from the large ships and other water vehicles. When this harmful substance just knock over to the water bodies then fish absorb it through it’s gills. Which causes harm to them. Another thing is oil also blocks the Sunlight from going water. As a result, this affects sea plants coral reefs, planktons and so on. Which results in collapse of Ecosystem. After that food chain contamination occur even after the water looks clear because oil remains in sand, rocks, sea floor and sediment. Bigger issue is when the fish and small insects under water eat toxic particles predators eat them and toxins goes all the food chain under water. Read More

Climate Change

In todays world climate change is one of the curse for human being. Some of the major effects of climate change on Marine life is rising Ocean Temperatures, Ocean Acidification, Strong stroms, Low Oxygen etc. Due to this dissolve shells of crabs, oysters and plankton. Fish change their season and place for giving birth their babies. Which cause change of the colour, skeleton and behaviour for the survival.
